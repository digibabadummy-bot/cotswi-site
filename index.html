About COTSWI Technologies

COTSWI Technologies is a women-managed, bootstrapped EMS startup from Coimbatore. We deliver end-to-end manufacturing services with high traceability, quality assurance, and quick turnaround times. Our focus is on innovation and partnership to bring cost-effective, high-quality solutions to life.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a preferred EMS partner in India through technology-driven, reliable, and sustainable manufacturing.

Mission: To empower Indian innovation with high-quality wiring harness, PCB, and assembly solutions through dedicated expertise and transparent collaboration.

Services & Capabilities

Wiring Harness

Customised harness solutions for automotive, industrial, and consumer applications with full traceability.

PCB Assembly

SMT & THT PCB assembly with quality testing, component sourcing, and documentation support.

Mechanical Assembly

Box builds, jigs, fixtures, cable integration, and reliability testing for complete EMS solutions.
