About COTSWI Technologies

COTSWI Technologies is a women-managed, bootstrapped EMS startup from Coimbatore, specialising in reliable and traceable wiring harness and PCB assemblies. We focus on innovation, quality, and faster delivery timelines — helping customers turn ideas into production-ready solutions.
